# Competitive Platforms Overview

## Hardware Platforms

### PlayerMaker
**Type**: Wearable shoe sensors with real-time motion tracking  
**Website**: playermaker.com  
**FIFA Certification**: Yes

**Key Capabilities**:
- 1000 Hz sampling rate (6-axis motion tracking)
- Technical: touches, passes, shots, kicking velocity, foot balance
- Physical: distance, speed, sprints, accelerations, direction changes
- Tactical: passing networks, possession analysis
- Gait analysis for injury prevention
- Automated video tagging
- Remote monitoring capabilities

**Target Market**: Youth academies, individual players ($199-299)

---

### Catapult Sports
**Type**: GPS & IMU wearables (professional grade)  
**Positioning**: Elite academies & professional clubs

**Key Capabilities**:
- GPS tracking for position/distance
- LPS for indoor training
- Heart rate monitoring
- Acceleration/deceleration metrics
- Workload management
- Injury risk prediction

**Pricing**: $500-2000+ per unit (enterprise licensing)

---

### STATSports
**Type**: FIFA-approved GPS vests  
**Positioning**: Prosumer segment

**Key Capabilities**:
- GPS performance tracking
- Personalized coaching programs
- Training drills & masterclasses
- Strength & nutrition guidance

**Pricing**: $199-399 (subscription model)
