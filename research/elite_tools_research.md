## 5. Elite Academy Methodologies

### Ajax TIPS Model
**Technique, Insight, Personality, Speed**

1. **Technique** - Ball control, dribbling, passing, shooting, tackling
2. **Insight** - Tactical awareness, spatial awareness, decision-making, positioning
3. **Personality** - Mental strength, attitude, determination, leadership
4. **Speed** - Physical speed, quick thinking, agility, reaction time

Key Points:
- 4-3-3 formation consistency from youth to first team
- Unique Ajax passport documenting accomplishments
- Creative, fast-paced, fair play style
- Focus on opponent's half

### La Masia (Barcelona) - Three Ps
**Possession, Position, Pressing**

1. **Possession** - Ball retention and control
2. **Position** - Spatial awareness and positioning
3. **Pressing** - High-intensity pressing

Key Points:
- Continuous monitoring and evaluation
- Technical, tactical, physical, mental development
- Small group training for maximized attention
- Same methodology from youth to first team
