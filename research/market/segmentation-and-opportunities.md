# Market Segmentation & Opportunities

## Geographic Markets

### North America (USA/Canada)

**Youth League Structure**:
- **MLS NEXT**: Elite pathway (151 clubs, 16,000+ players)
- **ECNL**: Elite Clubs National League
- **GA**: Girls Academy
- **NPL**: National Premier League
- **USL Youth**: Development league
- Local recreational leagues

**Market Characteristics**:
- Pay-to-play model dominant
- High parent involvement & expectations
- Strong college recruitment focus
- High technology adoption
- Performance data highly valued
- Professional pathway emphasis

**Key Platform Needs**:
- College exposure & recruiting tools
- Showcase event management
- Player recruiting profiles
- Performance tracking & analytics
- Parent communication portal
- Financial management & payments

---

### Europe (Spain/England)

**Academy Structure**:

- Independent academies
- Regional training centers
- Grassroots development programs

**Market Characteristics**:
- Integration with professional clubs

**Key Platform Needs**:
