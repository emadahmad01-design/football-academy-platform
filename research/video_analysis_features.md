## Source: TacticAI by DeepMind (https://deepmind.google/blog/tacticai-ai-assistant-for-football-tactics/)

### Key AI Features:

1. **Predictive Analysis**
   - Predict corner kick outcomes
   - Who is most likely to receive the ball
   - Shot attempt probability
   - Player movement prediction

2. **Generative Tactical Suggestions**
   - Sample alternative player setups
   - Evaluate possible outcomes of alternatives
   - Adjust tactics to make particular outcomes more likely
   - Reduce probability of shot attempts (defensive)

3. **Geometric Deep Learning**
   - Model player relations as graphs
   - Nodes represent players (position, velocity, height)
   - Edges represent relations between players
   - Reflection symmetry for better generalization

4. **Similar Play Retrieval**
   - Find similar corner kicks from history
   - Numerical player representations
   - 63% relevance rate for top-1 retrievals

5. **Counterfactual Analysis**
   - Generate alternative scenarios
   - Show how different positioning affects outcomes
   - Identify key players for tactic success/failure

### Key Insights for Our Platform:
- Show probability percentages for player actions
- Visualize player movement arrows and predictions
- Provide tactical recommendations
- Compare with similar past plays
- Generate "what-if" scenarios
