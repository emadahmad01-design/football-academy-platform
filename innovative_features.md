### 3. **Blockchain-Based Player Passport**
**Concept:** Immutable digital identity for player career tracking

**Features:**
- Birth certificate verification on blockchain
- All performance data stored permanently
- Medical history (injuries, treatments)
- Educational records
- Coaching certifications
- Transfer history
- Contract history
- Achievement NFTs (trophies, milestones)

**Market Gap:** No standardized, portable player identity system

**Benefits:**
- **For Players:** Portable career record, proof of achievements
- **For Academies:** Verified player history, fraud prevention
- **For Agents:** Transparent player representation
- **For Clubs:** Due diligence on player background

**Compliance:** GDPR-compliant, player-owned data
